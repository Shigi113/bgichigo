Case studies

POS + eTIMS

Olive Green Resort

Hospitality POS deployed across front-desk and restaurant points of sale, integrated with the KRA eTIMS invoicing pipeline. Manual billing was replaced with an automated, audit-ready checkout flow from day one.

  • Deployed & staff-trained in 4 days (on-site)
  • Handling 80+ transactions/day across two POS stations
  • eTIMS submission success rate raised to 99%+ with retry queue
  • Hardware integration: receipt printer, barcode scanner, cash drawer
  • Billing errors eliminated — shift reconciliation now takes <5 min

Full‑stack system

Digital Travellers SACCO

Built a custom accounting and member-management system for a transport SACCO, replacing fragmented spreadsheets with a centralised, role-controlled platform that gives management real-time visibility into finances.

  • Replaced 5+ manual spreadsheets with one unified system
  • Report generation time cut from hours → under 2 minutes
  • Full audit trail — every transaction stamped, traceable, exportable
  • Role-based access (admin, accountant, viewer) reduces data risk
  • Deployed on Linux VPS with automated daily backups
